# Evaluating-USDA-Food-Price-Outlook
Final project for GRAD 50500 - Foundations in Data Science
This project evaluates the accuracy and reliability of the USDA Economic Research Serviceâ€™s Food Price Outlook dataset. It compares forecasted food price changes against actual Consumer Price Index (CPI) outcomes, explores forecast errors by food category, and examines long-term inflation trends from the 1970s to the present.

### Datasets
Source: [USDA ERS Food Price Outlook - ](https://www.ers.usda.gov/data-products/food-price-outlook), [U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics - CUSR0000SA0](https://data.bls.gov/dataViewer/view/timeseries/CUSR0000SA0)
Formats: Excel, CSV
Data types: Numeric (percent changes), categorical (food group categories), temporal (year, month) 
Measurement formats:  
- Forecasted/actual CPI: Percent changes (%) in food prices
- Categories: Meats, dairy, cereals, fruits/vegetables, and overall food CPI
- Units: Percent change in Consumer Price Index (CPI)
- Granularity: Monthly and annual frequency
- Time Coverage: Historical CPI available from 1970s to present; forecasts available for upcoming months/years
- Geographic Coverage: United States
